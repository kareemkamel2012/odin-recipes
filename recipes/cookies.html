Cookies

Description

These triple chocolate chip cookies are 100% grandma-approved. Tantalizingly sweet and irresistibly chewy, they are the perfectly winter treat.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1 (3 oz) bar dark chocolate, cut into chunks
  • 3 tablespoons white chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Combine brown sugar, butter, white sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Beat until a creamy, deep brown mixture forms. Add egg and vanilla. Beat until mixture lightens and becomes smooth, 10 to 15 seconds.
  2. Mix flour and baking soda in a separate bowl. Add slowly to the wet ingredients until mostly incorporated. Fold in milk chocolate, dark chocolate, and white chcocolate using a spatula. Cover the dough and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, up to 48 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F when ready to bake.
  4. Divide cold dough into 2 1/4-ounce portions and place on a coookie sheet.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the edges start to look golden brown and crispy, 10 to 12 minutes. Do not overbake. Cool until cookies are set, aout 30 minutes.
