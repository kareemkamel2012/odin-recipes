Chicken Wings

Description

These flavorful grilled wings are sure to be a hit at any party or cookout, plus they make a great lunch any time. Easy to prepare and even easier to enjoy, you can't go wrong with these!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup Italian salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare wings: combine chicken wings, soy sauce, and Italian dressing in a large plastic bag, coat wings with marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal bag. Marinate in refrigerator, 4 hours to overnight.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Prepare sauce: melt butter in a small saucepan over low heat. Stir in hot sauce and 1 tsp soy sauce until combined. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Remove chicken wings from marinade and pat dry; discard remaining marinade. Cook on the grill, turning occasionally, until chicken is browned and no longer pink (25 to 30 minutes).
  5. Place grilled wings in a large bowl. Pour butter sauce over wings; toss until well-coated.
