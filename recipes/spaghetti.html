Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

Spaghetti and Meatballs is a classic dish originating from italy. The tasty noodles and the hearty ground beef compliment each other perfectly, along with some delicious sauce to complete the package.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • 1 large egg
  • table salt
  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until al dente in a large pot of salted boiling water, then drain.
  2. Mix beef with egg, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  3. Mix until combined and form into 16 balls.
  4. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add meatballs and cook for about 10 minutes (until browned on all sides), turning occasionally. Transfer meatballs to a plate.
  5. Add onion to pot and cook until soft, about 5 minutes. Add crushed tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper and bring to a simmer. Return meatballs to pot and cover. Simmer utnil sauce has thickened, 8 to 10 minutes.
  6. Serve pasta with a heaping scoop of meatballs and sauce. Top with Parmesan before serving.
